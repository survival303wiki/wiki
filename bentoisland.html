    • Bento Island

    Bento island is a starter island with unique animals, The Bento deer to be specific

    It is of a medium size and is ok for settlements

    Resources

    Bento contains Small trees, Large trees, Bushes, Berries, and small stones

    Bento island has a spawn but it has enouge space for a medium sized colony and enough resources for one

    Details

    Spawn:Yes

    Water:Lake

    Rocks and ores:Small stones

    Animals:Bento Deer, Bento Bucks

    Plants:Trees, Bushes, And berry bushes

    Survival tips

    Fishing and hunting should be fairly easy and if you decide to set up a village/fort

    Waterproof your things so people dont set it on fire.

    Make sure you can protect yourself also
